## Tweet 16
Etsy's "relevancy" ranking vs "recency" ranking:

New listings get a temporary visibility boost (recency). After that, it's all relevancy.

Relevancy = how well your title/tags/attributes match what buyers search.

Most sellers waste the recency window with weak titles. Fix your title BEFORE you publish, not after.

#EtsyTips #EtsySEO

## Tweet 17
The "13 tag" mistake I see in almost every struggling Etsy shop:

Tags like: "handmade" "gift" "cute" "unique" "beautiful"

These describe how YOU feel about your product. Buyers don't search these words.

Tags that work: "birthday gift for sister" "minimalist desk decor" "personalized teacher gift"

Describe the buyer's search intent, not your product's qualities.

## Tweet 18
How Etsy search actually works (simplified):

1. Buyer types query
2. Etsy matches query to title + tags + attributes
3. From matched listings, Etsy ranks by: conversion rate, recency, shipping speed, shop quality score
4. You see results sorted by "relevancy"

Most sellers only optimize step 2 (matching). The best sellers optimize all 4 steps.

#EtsySEO

## Tweet 19
