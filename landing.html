Frequently Asked Questions

Is Listora's keyword data real or made up? ▶
Listora combines Google search data (what real users search for when looking for Etsy products) with AI-powered scoring to estimate volume, competition, and opportunity. While we don't scrape Etsy's internal search database directly, our keyword intelligence is grounded in real search behavior and provides actionable guidance for your listings.
How is Listora different from eRank or Marmalead? ▶
eRank and Marmalead are primarily data tools — they show you keyword numbers but don't write anything. Listora is a complete listing creation tool: it researches keywords AND writes your titles, descriptions, and tags using that data. Plus, our Competitor Analysis and Bulk Optimizer features don't exist anywhere else at this price point.
Will Listora violate Etsy's terms of service? ▶
No. Listora is an SEO research and writing tool. It doesn't interact with Etsy's platform, access your account, or automate any actions on Etsy. You copy the generated content and manually paste it into your listings — exactly like using any other writing tool. This is completely within Etsy's terms.
How fast will I see results? ▶
Most sellers see improved impressions within 1-2 weeks of updating their listings. Significant ranking improvements typically happen within 30-60 days as Etsy's algorithm re-indexes your listings. The Competitor Analysis tool often shows the fastest results since it targets keywords with proven demand.
Can I cancel anytime? ▶
Yes. Cancel anytime with no questions, no cancellation fees, and no penalty. If you cancel within 30 days, we'll give you a full refund — no questions asked.
Does the Bulk Optimizer work for large shops? ▶
Yes! The Bulk Optimizer lets you optimize up to 20 listings per run. Pro users get unlimited runs, so you can optimize your entire shop in batches. Each listing gets a full AI-generated title (3 variants), description, and 13 tags.
